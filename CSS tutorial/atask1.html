Welcome to Asian Group

Asian Overseas is a foremost house of textile processing in India and relishes its broad process capacity. We are engaged in the textile processing with offering of value addition for
all types of fabrics, together with cotton, polyester, poly-cotton, poly-viscose, glazed cotton etc. We present ground-breaking textile value addition solutions on all kinds of fabrics namely Pleating, Crushing, Flocking, Embossing, Crinkling and Shimmering, foil printing on rotary machine, pigment print, khari print, rubber print, shimmering etc on all types of fabrics. We also have a dedicated unit for automatic panel printing.
